What does "page navigation section" mean to you?

The current system has small 10×10px dots at the bottom (only visible on NINA instance pages), swipe gestures, and a physical button. Let's clarify scope before designing.

A

Just the Page Indicator Dots

Redesign the bottom dot indicators to be more informative and visually appealing. Keep swipe gestures and button as-is.

B

Full Navigation Experience

Rethink dots, page discovery, and how users access ALL pages (including settings, sysinfo, AllSky, Spotify) — not just NINA instances. Reduce the "hidden page" problem where dots disappear.

C

New Navigation Paradigm

Replace swiping with a persistent nav bar, drawer, or dock — something always visible that gives direct access to any page in one tap.
